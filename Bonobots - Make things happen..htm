What is Bonobots?

Bonobots is a social network to manage your activities, based on smart and customized objects, to follow, interact and coach people in your community.

Playing with Bonobots means:

  • Create, share or join activities in your own profile or in a micro community.

  • Push for achievment of your activities, through targeted notifications, decision-making data filtering, interactions with the targetted people, chronological follow-up.

  • Get a global scope on your activities, through graphs and data board.

  • Make yours and optimize Bonobots objects behaviors, related to your needs, your experience, your goals. Bring your value-added to the whole community.

Concept

Social networks could interact with people in a more personnal and dynamic way through notifications, alerts, invitation and data-sharing. The idea is to give the users the tool to design the interactions that they prefer, so they can target their needs and what they expect from the system.

Bonobot "Swimming Follow Up With Coach".
This bonobot lets you choose a coach among your community. If the training is not confirmed 30 minutes before it starts, the bonobot notifies the coach and suggests him/her to send you back a message through the application, to motivate you.
Once the activity is over, the coach gets access to the results filled by the swimmer.
The bonobot is your perfect activity manager. It has its own behavior, designed for a particular activity. It can send notifications, change itself its status, display and analyze many data, and send you back the exact information you need.

Bonobots can be used for one particular activity with a precise workflow, like "Swimming training" or "Bar in Paris", but also for simple activities, like "personnal reminder" or "meeting".

All the bonobots are referenced and rated in a public listing. They can be used right away!

Build Your Own

The "Builder" lets you create your own customized bonobots, based on what you need. Go step by step and define:

  • the data checked by the bonobot,
  • the rules to define when the bonobot should do something,
  • the actions it can take,
  • the scenarios, connecting the rules to the actions.

"Flight" Simulator

Flight Simulator.
The Flight Simulator is a tool to test the behavior of the bonobots in a sandbox. "Does the bonobot I just designed do what I expect?"

It describes how the bonobot is analyzing the data through time and user interactions. Each bonobot in the public listing can also be opened in the Flight Simulator. This is helpful to understand how it is working, so you can get inspired to conceive yours.

Each new bonobot must be validated through this Flight Simulator before being released in the public listing. The tool will let you know what is wrong in case of non-validation, so it can be fixed easily.
